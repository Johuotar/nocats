Himon Vangit

Himon Vangit is eclectic-effective-electronic music and art collective from Turku and Naantali (Finland). Himon Vangit (Prisoners of Desire) offers progressive space disco and electro that can send you to distant sfääris (spheres AKA planets). Jumps into hyperspace, maneuvers among pleiades and landings to unknown alien worlds are everyday for these musicians! -Official announcement (translation by me)

Garoad

Garoad has released several EP's and has made soundtracks for games such as Yuppie Psycho and VA-11 Hall-A: Cyberpunk Bartender Action. The genres include such as vaporwave, synthwave, chiptune and electronica.

"Michael "Garoad" Kelly is a freelance video game composer. While flexible with many styles of music, Michael is best known for his blend of Japanese Anime, PS1, and PS2 era influences."-Official profile description

Josh Whelchel

Josh Whelchel is composer, producer and engineer. He has made soundtracks for games such as The Spirit Engine series, Masquerada: Songs and Shadows, Kromaia and Scrolls. He has also produced music for the electronic band called Poolside.

"In 2003, I had the great opportunity of working with Mark Pay to create The Spirit Engine & The Spirit Engine 2. These projects set off a career of writing music for video games, which connected me with Mojang [Scrolls], Mattias Häggström Gerdt [Anosou], Witching Hour Studios [Masquerada & Ravenmark], Adult Swim Games [Oblitus], and several others." -Official description
